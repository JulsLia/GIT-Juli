Vacation in the Maldives. Make a choice and enjoy an unforgettable vacation in the Maldives.

Vacation on the island resort

The most popular type of vacation in the Maldives. Various hotels with all amenities and infrastructure for a comfortable stay of guests are located on the resort islands. Entertainment, excursions and services of your choice. Depending on individual preferences and price request, you can choose your own suitable island.

Wild Maldives

A less common, but increasingly popular type of budget vacation in the Maldives. The advantages are that during the trip you will be able to visit and live on several "local" islands. As a rule, these are small villages with the necessary infrastructure: shops, cafes, pharmacy, beaches and other. Get acquainted with the culture and life of the real Maldives, because locals live on the islands. Accommodation in a mini-hotel or guest house. There are also options for activities such as excursions, fishing, snorkeling and more.

Maldives Yacht Cruise

A yacht cruise is a unique opportunity to diversify your vacation and discover the Maldives and the Indian Ocean from a new side. Explore uninhabited islands, go diving and just admire the beauty of the island state from aboard a luxury ship. The programs and routes of cruises are diverse. Cruises lasting from 3 to 10 days. The program includes diving, snorkeling, fishing, recreation on the most beautiful sandy beaches, picnics on uninhabited islands and night parties on the ship. Accommodation and meals on board.
